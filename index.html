Privacy Policy for Soonish

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Soonish is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what the app does — and does not — collect.

Information we collect

None. Soonish does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Data you create

Events you add — their titles, dates, colors, and reminder settings — are stored only on your device (including your device's shared app storage, which powers the home-screen widget). This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Notifications

If you turn on reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device by iOS. No notification content is sent to any server.

Third parties

Soonish uses no third-party services, SDKs, advertising, or analytics.

Children's privacy

Soonish does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
